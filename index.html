Parallelized engineers

Parallelized Engineers: Sci-Fi Building Adventure

Original Concept: Created for Connect 23 Game Jam with, theme 'parallelism' by Unviere.

- 📝 story mode: Prof Unviere is lost after creating a portal. Explore the Great Plain, interact with residents, and uncover her disappearance. Discover she’s been kidnapped by one of the residents.

- ⚔️ Fight Mode: Travel dangerous dimensions, build forts to survive against creatures, and trust no one.

- 🎨 Creative Mode: Build and explore with no limits

- 💀 Hardcore Mode: Survive with one live

- party: connect with friends or others to build together

- 🌌 Sci-Fi Theme: Use futuristic tools from Prof.

Alpha Version: Playable for testing; some systems may not be fully functional. Open Beta test one soon
